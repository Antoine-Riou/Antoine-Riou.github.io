PHONE BOOTH02

Phone Booth is a gripping thriller unfolding entirely within the tight confines of a phone booth.

For a school project, I crafted a 3D title sequence capturing that intense, claustrophobic atmosphere.
I modeled the booth in Cinema 4D using film references and added gritty handset textures in Photoshop.
The animation included Z-depth passes and was polished in After Effects with color grading and cinematic effects to amplify the suspense.
