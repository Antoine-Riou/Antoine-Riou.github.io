REPLICA 200201

Replica 2002 are sneakers inspired by the shape of the Formula 2002 and made by the creative brand Thug Club.

The idea was to merge car and shoe animation into one dynamic visual project.
From modeling a high-quality sole in 3D to animating cars and simulating smoke, I handled multiple steps of a full 3D production pipeline.
I also designed the title graphics in Illustrator and did the compositing in After Effects, including Z-depth, glow, and custom transition effects to polish the final look.
