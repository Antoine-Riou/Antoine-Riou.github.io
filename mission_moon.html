MISSION TO THE MOON03

The MoonSwatch Mission to the Moon is a watch born from the iconic collaboration between Swatch and Omega, celebrating lunar exploration and space travel.

For this project, I created a commercial video blending macro B-roll shots of the watch with stylized 2D/3D animation of a rocket launch heading to the Moon.
I filmed the watch underwater in my aquarium, using controlled lighting and reflections to evoke a deep, otherworldly atmosphere.
Celestial elements like Earth, Moon, stars, and engine flames were designed in Photoshop and After Effects.
The rocket animation and scene assembly were done in Cinema 4D, with final rendering and compositing in After Effects, including glow, shadows, and voice-over to complete the story.
